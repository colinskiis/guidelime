## Scope

Diagnosis, classification, and management of gestational hypertension and preeclampsia including monitoring, antihypertensive therapy, seizure prophylaxis, and timing of delivery.

## Notes

Replaces Practice Bulletin 202. Hypertensive disorders are a leading cause of maternal and perinatal mortality worldwide. Includes criteria for preeclampsia with and without severe features.
