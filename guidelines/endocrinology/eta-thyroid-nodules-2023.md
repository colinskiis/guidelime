## Scope

European approach to evaluation and management of thyroid nodules including ultrasound classification (EU-TIRADS), fine needle aspiration indications, molecular testing, and management of indeterminate cytology.

## Notes

European perspective complementing the ATA 2015 guidelines. Uses EU-TIRADS classification system. Incorporates updated evidence on molecular testing and active surveillance.
