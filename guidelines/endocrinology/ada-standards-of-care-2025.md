## Scope

Comprehensive annual guideline covering all aspects of diabetes care including diagnosis, classification, prevention, glycemic targets, pharmacologic and non-pharmacologic management, complications, and special populations.

## Notes

Updated annually by ADA Professional Practice Committee. 2025 updates include: CGM consideration for T2D on glucose-lowering agents, resistance training guidance for those on weight management therapy, updated DKA/HHS guidance, screening for fear of hypoglycemia and diabetes distress. Endorsed by ACC (Section 10), American Geriatrics Society (Section 13), ASBMR (bone health), and Obesity Society (Section 8).
