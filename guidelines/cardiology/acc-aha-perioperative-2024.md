## Scope

Comprehensive recommendations for perioperative cardiovascular risk assessment and management in patients undergoing noncardiac surgery, including preoperative evaluation, risk calculators, diagnostic testing, and perioperative medical therapy.

## Notes

Major update to 2014 guideline. Includes sections on epidemiology, risk calculators, preoperative cardiovascular diagnostic testing, cardiovascular comorbidities, perioperative medical therapy, anesthetic considerations, and perioperative surveillance.
