## Scope

Comprehensive guideline for diagnosis, risk stratification, and management of atrial fibrillation including stroke prevention, rate and rhythm control strategies, and catheter ablation.

## Notes

First major update since 2014 (focused update in 2019). Key updates include expanded indications for catheter ablation, updated anticoagulation recommendations, and new classification of AF stages.
