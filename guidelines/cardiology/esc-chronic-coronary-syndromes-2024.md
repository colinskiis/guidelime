## Scope

Diagnosis and management of patients with chronic coronary syndromes including diagnostic evaluation, risk stratification, lifestyle and pharmacological management, and revascularization decisions.

## Notes

Updates the 2019 CCS guideline. Developed by 28 task force members including 26 experts and 2 patients.
