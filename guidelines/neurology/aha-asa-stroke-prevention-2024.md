## Scope

Comprehensive guideline for primary prevention of both ischemic and hemorrhagic stroke in individuals with no history of stroke, including risk factor management and screening recommendations.

## Notes

Replaces 2014 primary prevention guideline. Addresses modifiable risk factors including hypertension, diabetes, dyslipidemia, atrial fibrillation, and lifestyle factors.
