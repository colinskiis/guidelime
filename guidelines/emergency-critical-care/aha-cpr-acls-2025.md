## Scope

Comprehensive guidelines for adult, pediatric, and neonatal cardiopulmonary resuscitation and emergency cardiovascular care including BLS, ACLS, PALS, and post-cardiac arrest care.

## Notes

Major update with 760 specific recommendations. Key changes: unified 6-link Chain of Survival (consolidated from previous separate chains), updated choking response (5 back blows alternating with 5 abdominal thrusts), mechanical CPR not routinely recommended but may be considered in specific situations.
