## Scope

Comprehensive international guidelines for the management of sepsis and septic shock in adults, covering screening, diagnosis, initial resuscitation, antimicrobial therapy, source control, hemodynamic management, ventilation, and long-term outcomes.

## Notes

Major update with increased emphasis on post-ICU care and long-term outcomes. Greater geographic and gender diversity than previous versions. Sponsored by SCCM and ESICM with no industry funding.
