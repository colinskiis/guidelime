## Scope

Evidence-based strategy for COPD prevention, diagnosis, management, and exacerbation prevention including pharmacologic and non-pharmacologic therapies.

## Notes

Revised annually each November (World COPD Day). 2025 updates include three new sections on cardiovascular risk, climate change, and pulmonary hypertension in COPD patients.
